Grilled Cheese

Description

These crustless grilled cheese sandwiches are great to have stashed in the freezer for when the kids need a quick snack.
Easy for little hands to hold, with the cheese sealed inside and no tough crusts, they're fun for them and convenient for you. You will need a sandwich sealer for this recipe, and they are easy to find online

Ingredients

  • 8 slices white sandwich bread
  • 4 slices Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons softened butter, or as needed

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Cut round slices of cheese with a cookie cutter and place one on each of 4 bread slices (buttered side down). Top with the remaining bread slices (buttered side up)
  3. Use a sandwich sealer to press and seal the sandwiches. Discard crusts
  4. Freeze the sandwiches on a parchment-lined baking sheet for 2 hours, then transfer to a freezer bag
